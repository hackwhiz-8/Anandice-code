ICRDET AWARDS

Best Paper Awards:

Criteria: The Best Paper Presentation award will be determined based on the originality, significance, and clarity of the research presented. Additionally, the effectiveness of the presentation, including communication skills and engagement with the audience, will also be considered.
Selection Process: A designated panel of judges, comprising esteemed experts in the field, will evaluate presentations during the conference sessions. Each judge will independently assess the presentations based on the predetermined criteria.
Announcement: The winner of the Best Paper Presentation award will be announced during the closing ceremony of the conference. The recipient will be presented with a Certificate of recognition as a token of appreciation.
