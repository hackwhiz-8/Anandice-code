Call for Papers:

ICRDET-2024 is focused on UN approved the 2030 Agenda, indicating 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). The purpose of this conference is to provide a leading forum for the presentation of new advances and research that focuses on innovative advancements and sustainability in Engineering and Technology. It helps experts, researchers, and industry professionals worldwide to come together to share knowledge and shape the future of engineering sustainably. Accordingly, Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:
