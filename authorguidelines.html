Welcome Message:

Dear Esteemed Participants,
As the Provost of the Lebanese American University and Honorary Chair of the Organizing Committee, it is my pleasure to welcome you to the seventh edition of the International Conference on Mathematical Modelling, Applied Analysis, and Computation ( ICACDSA-2025 ) in Beirut, Lebanon.
ICACDSA-2025 continues its mission to facilitate collaboration between practitioners and theoreticians in applied mathematics, addressing both theoretical and applied aspects of mathematical modeling. The conference encompasses a wide spectrum of topics, including differential equations, fractional differential equations, distribution functions, fuzzy mathematical models, and dynamic systems, covering deterministic, stochastic, continuous, discrete, or hybrid systems concerning time. Additionally, the conference explores applied analysis, utilizing analytical and numerical techniques to address challenges in science, engineering, and finance.
This year, ICACDSA-2025 will adopt a hybrid format, merging virtual and in-person experiences. Hosted in Beirut, a city celebrated for its cultural richness, the conference aims to deliver a comprehensive and engaging program. Despite the hybrid nature, our commitment to delivering an academically enriching conference remains unwavering. The program offers in-person participants the opportunity to immerse themselves in Lebanese culture and scenery. Lebanon, with its renowned hospitality, diverse sites, and culinary offerings, provides an ideal backdrop for this intellectual gathering.
We encourage you to embrace the academic exchange at ICACDSA-2025 , renew professional connections, and support the participation of junior academics and research students in this conference.
I trust that ICACDSA-2025 will be a valuable and enriching experience for all participants.
Sincerely,
George E. Nasr
Provost
Lebanese American University
Honorary Chair of ICMMAAC 2024
